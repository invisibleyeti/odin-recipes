Instant Pot Curry

Description

This is the recipe my partner makes all the time. This curry is super easy and comes out amazing every time. As far as prep work goes you cut and peel all the vegtables into bite sized bites. Then you mince the ginger and garlic. Saute the ginger and garlic in the instant pot for a min or 2 before adding the rest of the ingredients(besides kethup and soy) Then you cook it for 15 mintues in the instant pot on high. After that you vent the instant pot and then add ketchup and the soy while mixing it all in. Then you put the curry on some rice and you are good to go.

Ingredients

  • 3 onions
  • 1 1/2 carrots
  • 3 yukon golden potatotes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 1/2 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/8 tsp fresh ground pepper
  • 1 Tbsp neutral oil

For the Curry Sauce

  • 3 cups chickn stock/broth
  • 1 package Japanese curry roux
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
