Oyakodon (Chicken and Egg Bowl

Description

This is a recipe that I don't make in an instant pot. Instead it uses one pan and it really fast. This only makes one portion though so if you want to cook for multiple peoplle it can be a tad time consuming. Bascially you Cook onion and chicken in a sauce that you make. Then you add egg to it and so that it all cooks into this delicious dish that goes on top of rice. The egg absorbs the some of the sauce making it less watery and so freaking tasty.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 large eggs

For the Sauce

  • 1/2 cup dashi
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Mirin
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
