Instant Pot Tacos

Description

This is my favorite recipe for when I'm in a rush and tired. Basically you have and instant pot you throw in all of the ingredients into the instant pot then you cook on high for 12 minutes. After that you just depressurize the pot when its done. Then you shred the chicken with 2 forks and put it back into the sauce and stir. Then you get your taco shells or tortillas, and whatever toppings you want and make some healthy and tasty tacos that took you less than 10 minutes of labor.

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons taco Seasoning
  • 1/3 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup salsa

For Serving

  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • cheese
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
